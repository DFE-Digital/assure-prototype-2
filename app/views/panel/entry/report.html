How to write the report

As an assessor you need to think about what the team has done well and any recommendations, so they can be added to the report.

It's the lead assessor's responsibility to add these to the final report and submit it.

As a panel you also need to decide on an overall outcome for the peer review.

Work together in a way that suits you best

You could:

  • collaborate as a team in a shared Word doc before the lead assessor adds the content to the report
  • use the panel chat in Slack to collaborate on findings and recommendations. The lead assessor then consolidates and adds them to the report

Guidance to help you draft the report

Read the following:

  • complete the peer review report
  • consider standards 1 to 7 in the discovery phase

What happens after the report is submitted

The lead assessor submits the report to the service assessment team.

They will check it then send it to the delivery team.
